Lasagna

Description to Yummiest Lasagna

Creamy lasagna with italian natural herbs, peppers, cheese, sauce and seasoning

Ingredients

  • pasta sheets
  • ricotta
  • mozzarella

Steps to Yummiest Lasagna

  1. Spread a layer of tomato-based sauce
  2. Add layers of pasta sheets
  3. Add a layer of white sauce
  4. Followed by layer of pasta sheets
  5. Add toppings
  6. Bake at 650 degrees for 45 minutes
  7. Let it cool down
  8. Serve hot
