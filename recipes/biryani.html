Biryani

Description to Yummiest Biryani

Authentic indian biryani with natural indian herbs and organic meat

Ingredients

  • Indian spices
  • Rice
  • Meat

Steps to Yummiest Biryani

  1. Take one cup full rice
  2. Cook meat aside
  3. Add indian spices to meat
  4. Add boiled rice to it
  5. Let it steam for 40 minutes at 450 degrees
  6. Serve hot the aromatic biryani
