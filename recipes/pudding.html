Description to Yummiest Pudding

Chia Seed Pudding with organic chia seeds, greek yogurt and fruits

Ingredients

  • Non-citrus fruits
  • Chia Seeds
  • Greek Yogurt

Steps to Yummiest Pudding

  1. Take one cup full chia seeds
  2. Add 4 cups of yogurt
  3. Add unsweetened milk
  4. Stir well
  5. Add chopped fruits as toppings
  6. Place in the fridge overnight
  7. Serve with nuts and peanut butter
